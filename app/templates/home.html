Welcome to LeedsLinked

Your University of Leeds Community

LeedsLinked is an exclusive online community tailored for the vibrant student body of the University of Leeds. Whether you're a first-year navigating campus life, a final-year student facing the challenges of dissertation season, or anyone in between, this hub is your go-to destination for all things Leeds.

What Makes LinkedLeeds Special:

  • Subject-Specific Blogs: Dive into blogs tailored to your specific courses and subjects by searching them. Connect with peers studying the same modules, share study tips.
  • Leeds Life: Explore the best of Leeds beyond the lecture halls. Discover local events, favorite hangout spots, and recommendations from fellow students. From hidden gems to must-visit places, this is your guide to the city.
  • University Affairs: Stay in the loop with the latest university news, events, and announcements. Discuss campus developments, student organizations, and opportunities to get involved in the University of Leeds community.
  • Accommodation Insights: Whether you're a resident of university accommodation or navigating the private housing market, exchange advice, ask questions, and share your experiences with finding the perfect place to call home in Leeds.
  • Career Corner: Connect with students pursuing similar career paths, seek advice from alumni, and stay informed about career-related events and opportunities specific to the University of Leeds.

How to Get Involved:

  • Add To Your Profile: Create a profile and join our community! Share a bit about yourself, your course, and your interests. Connect with other students.
  • Engage in Blogging: Give your opinon by blogging on what's happening across University. Ask questions, offer insights, and be part of a supportive community that understands the unique journey of a University of Leeds student.
  • Stay Updated: Look through blogs and learn about important university updates, upcoming events. Tailor your experience to ensure you're always in the know.
  • Give your opinon: Like or dislike blogs to share what you think about what people are saying out there
